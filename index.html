About

From Microbes to SQL to Swift:
A self-driven path in tech.

Hi, there.

So I've made a decade long career out of wrangling data, where most of it was spent supporting the backend of a SaaS product. I found myself in IT by taking a couple of database courses for my first corporate job out of college.

I learned about web development on the fly for work and basic curiousity. I learned my first programming language, Swift, by reading its documentation.

I am a serial autodidact and love sharing freely available knowledge, hence the somewhat popular Github repository, Open Source Mathematics Degree.

I love helping others build their online presence and to inspire others to get into tech. I also have a YouTube channel where I share programming language topics that I'm learning or find interesting, plus a couple of tutorials.

I'm currently obsessed with building my own SaaS in Python and learning how to teach machines (generative AI).
